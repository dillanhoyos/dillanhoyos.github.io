Biography

Dillan Hoyos is a Musician/ Producer that focuses on the creation of different projects related to music. Everything from Sound Design, Music Production and Performing arts. With a really broad set of influences, borned in Houston, TX and raised in Bogota,Colombia, Began his studies on 2017 at Berklee College of Music, Majoring in Guitar Performance and Electronic Production. Very passionate about Music and its creative process. Interested in different aspects of the industry such as new implementation of design for User interaction with music, Virtual reality audio, and live sound innovations.

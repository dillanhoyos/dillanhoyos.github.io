DreamMachine

Hi there!

My name is Dillan Hoyos and I am a Sound Designer with three years of experience in the video game industry. I have a passion for Audio engineering and strive to bring my best effort to every project I work on.

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work with a wide range of clients and have developed a diverse skill set as a result. Some of my areas of expertise include Sound Design, Technical Audio implementation and scripting such as: C#, Javascript, C++ and visual scripting (Blueprints, Pure Data, Max/Msp, Reaktor).

I am always looking for new opportunities to grow and improve, and I am excited to share my portfolio with you. Thank you for taking the time to visit my website and learn more about me and my work.

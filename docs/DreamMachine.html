Dream Machine

The Dream Machine Project is a multi-player, VR, interactive platform that provides a unique stage for artists to showcase their music and educate players about their musical influences in an engaging and entertaining manner. In this project, I served as a Unity Developer and Technical Audio Designer. My responsibilities included the development and implementation of various technical aspects of the project using Unity game engine. Additionally, I was responsible for the technical design and implementation of audio for the project. The technologies used in this project included NormCore and Wwise, which served as the middleware for audio management and integration. These technologies were instrumental in delivering a seamless and immersive experience for the users and ensured that the audio design was of the highest quality.









Twin Edge

The Twin Edge Rhythm Game presents players with a unique and exciting challenge, requiring them to master the art of tapping on two separate reels to progress through the levels. As a technical Audio Implementer and Musician, I was fortunate enough to play a crucial role in the development of this game. Utilizing Unity game engine, I was responsible for ensuring that the audio aspects of the game were implemented to the highest technical standards. My role as a musician involved composing and incorporating a fitting musical score that complemented the rhythm-based gameplay. The result was a seamless and immersive audio experience that enhanced the overall enjoyment of the game for players. It was an exciting and rewarding experience to bring my technical and musical skills to the forefront in the creation of the Twin Edge Rhythm Game.









Mindfuel

Minduel is a Virtual Reality platform designed to provide a comfortable and immersive meditation experience for users. I played a crucial role in its development as a solo Unity developer, responsible for the creation of the platform's environments, mechanics, and programming. In addition, I served as the Sound Designer, creating audio elements to enhance the calming atmosphere and promote deeper relaxation. My goal was to ensure that Minduel was not only aesthetically pleasing but also effective in promoting mental well-being. I am proud to have contributed to a platform that offers a unique and convenient solution for individuals seeking to improve their mental and emotional health.





Dead Tired

Dead Tired is a puzzling adventure that'll have you tapping, swiping, and thinking your way to victory! This game was crafted with love and creativity, and it's no wonder that it won first place in level design at the GJL game jam! As a technical Sound Designer, I was lucky enough to be part of this talented team. I was tasked with adding the auditory magic that brought this game to life. Every tap, swipe, and solving of a puzzle was accompanied by satisfying sounds that enhanced the gameplay experience.

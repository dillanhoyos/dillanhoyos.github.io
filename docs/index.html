Dillan Hoyos is a Music Technologist that focuses in Creative Audio Projects. Graduated From Berklee College Of Music Cum Laude for the Program of Electronic Production and Design with Emphasis in creative Coding
He has been participating in different musical Projects such Whale tail band on 2018 as a guitar player and assisted artists as a Recording Engineer such as FM Collective since then he has been focusing on creating Audio Content for Media and implementing Sounds on Video Games.

Participated on the MIT Reality Hackathon on January of 2020, Designed and created Sounds for a AR application that raised awareness on Plastic contamination.
Since then he has been participating on the creation of Indie games in which he has released two of them on Itch.io. He is really passionate about the creative process and the Design Mindset. Some Current projects he is developing is a Musical Sample Library called IndeXsounds in which he is the Founder of this creative Project.
